⚔️ Mining mode vs Miner Wars
Same TH/s, two completely different games. Mining mode is the predictable BTC drip — paid daily, full discount stack. Miner Wars is a weekly clan competition with an excess-fee tax on earnings above your solo ceiling, calculated at the league's average W/TH (worse than yours if you're efficient). This calculator shows you which one actually pays more for your specific setup.

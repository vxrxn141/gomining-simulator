About GoMining Simulator

A free profitability calculator and strategy explorer
for GoMining NFT miners.

GoMining Simulator (gmsim.ca) is an independent web tool that helps NFT miners on the GoMining platform model their returns, compare strategies, and track real-world performance. We are not affiliated with GoMining, its operators, or any exchange. We're just two crypto enthusiasts who got tired of doing the math in spreadsheets.

Free, forever

The core simulator and calculator are free to use. The Site is supported by display advertising served by Google AdSense. We may launch a premium tier with extra features down the road, but the basics will always remain free.

Privacy-first

We collect the minimum needed to operate the Site (your email, basic usage stats). We never ask for wallet keys, seed phrases, or anything that could compromise your funds. Read the full privacy policy for the details.

Open about who we are

GoMining Simulator is built and maintained by two people — one based in Canada, one in the UAE. We mine on GoMining ourselves, which is how the project started. We use the same tool you use.
